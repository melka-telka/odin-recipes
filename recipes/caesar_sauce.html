Caesar Sauce without Mayo

Description

Home-made Caesar sauce without mayo. Tastes the same as traditional Caesar, but with way less calories.

Ingridients

  • 1 cup of Greek yoghurt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp mustard
  • 1 tbsp Worchester sauce
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Steps

  1. Simply mix all of the ingridients above in a pot
  2. Serve and enyoj!

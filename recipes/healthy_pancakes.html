Healthy pancakes

Description

Love pancakes but trying to lose weight? Try out this recipe for a delicious, guilt-free experience!

The best part? You need only a handful of ingridients that you proabably already have in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 Banana
  • 40g Oatmeal
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tbsp Baking Powder
  • 1 tspn Vanilla Extract
  • 1 tspn Coconut Oil
  • Optional: 80ml Almond Milk

Steps

  1. In a bowl, smash the banana with a fork
  2. Add the eggs and the oatmeal and mix everything together
  3. Add the baking powder and vanilla extract to the mix
  4. Put a tspn of coconut oil into a pan and add the mix
  5. Fry for about 2 min per side
  6. Serve and enjoy!

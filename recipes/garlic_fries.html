Garlic Fries with Chives

Description

Oven-baked garlic fries! No pan, no frying, no fats. Just the sweet taste of garlicy potatoes sprinkeld with chives

Ingredients

  • 3 potatoes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Garlic salt
  • Chives

Steps

  1. Cut the potatoes and place in tray
  2. Season with salt and garlic salt
  3. Put in oven at 200 degrees for 30 min
  4. Sprinkle with garlic salt and chives
  5. Enjoy!
